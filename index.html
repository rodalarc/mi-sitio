“Sulfate Leaching Poses Environmental Challenge in North Aridia’s Mining Region”

North Aridia, 21 July 2025 – Environmental scientists warn of increasing sulfate contamination in groundwater around the Redstone Mine, after multi-isotopic analysis (δ³⁴S and δ¹⁸O) revealed accelerated sulfate mobilization from mine tailings.
